Hi, I'm **Madne Girnari** — a Senior Data Analyst with **8+ years** in audit analytics, financial risk, and enterprise data engineering at leading banking institutions.

At **IDFC FIRST Bank**, I work at the intersection of internal audit and AI: I built **STARK**, an enterprise GenAI utility powered by GPT-4o that turns plain-language requirements into audit-grade SQL, and I run ML pipelines on Cloudera/HDFS that scan millions of banking transactions daily for anomalies.

My toolkit: **Python, PySpark, SQL, SAS**, Power BI, and cloud-native data platforms (AWS, Cloudera). Domain depth in internal audit analytics, liquidity risk (LCR/NSFR), and core banking systems.

I like problems where messy financial data has to become something a risk committee can act on — dashboards, triggers, models, or a well-timed anomaly flag.
